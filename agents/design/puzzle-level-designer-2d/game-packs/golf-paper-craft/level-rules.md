# Level rules — Golf Paper Craft

## L1–L5 spec (game-pack)

These are specific onboarding targets and should not appear in core knowledge.

| L | Distance | Obstacle | maxShots | New mechanic | Canonical solve | Completion target | Avg attempt |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| L1 "Baslangic" | 6-8 | 0 | 5 | aim + power (flat shot) | 1 shot, mid power | 98% | 1.1 |
| L2 "Hafif egim" | 8-10 | 15° slope | 4 | slope affects power | 1-2 shots | 92% | 1.4 |
| L3 "Yukseklik" | 10-12 | elevated hole | 4 | arc control | 1-2 arc shots | 85% | 2.0 |
| L4 "Ilk engel" | 12-14 | single wall (hole behind) | 3 | combine: arc+power | 2 shots | 78% | 2.5 |
| L5 "Trampoline izole" | 12-15 | trampoline + wall | 3 | trampoline mandatory (isolated intro) | 2 shots, bounce | 72% | 3.0 |

## L1 checklist
- Ball visible, hole visible, no obstacle between, no scroll
- Weak shot still reaches near the hole (near miss teaches)
- First-shot success probability intentionally ~30–50%
- UI: single gesture (drag-aim), no panels

## L2 checklist
- Slope appears beside path first, then under the path
- Failure penalty remains ~0 (fast retry)

## Iteration rule
Every level must be tested with 10+ players; time-to-success median should stay within ±50% band. Outside band = redesign.
